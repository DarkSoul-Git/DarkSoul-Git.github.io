Privacy

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit the website https://darksoul-git.github.io/.

Last updated: October 12, 2023.

Information We Collect

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device. We collect this data using cookies, log files, and other tracking technologies. This information may include your IP address, browser type, internet service provider, referring/exit pages, your operating system, date/time stamps, and related data. We refer to this automatically collected information as "Device Information."

Cookies

We use cookies on our website for advertising and understanding on how the users interact with our site.

Github Cookies

Please note that our website, hosted on GitHub, uses cookies to provide you with a secure and improved browsing experience. These cookies play essential roles, such as Enhancing Security, Gathering Insights, Supporting Future Development and so on. For detailed information about the specific cookies used, their purposes, and expiration, you can find at GitHub Cookie Policy page, which outlines these details.

To know more about Privacy Policies of GitHub, visit at GitHub Privacy Statement page.

Google Adsense

  • Our website utilizes third-party vendors, such as Google, which employ cookies to display advertisements tailored to a user's previous visits to our site.
  • Google's utilization of advertising cookies empowers both Google and its partners to present advertisements to our users that are based on their interactions with our website or other websites across the internet.
  • Users have the option to manage their preferences regarding these advertising cookies by visiting their Ad Settings. This enables them to control their exposure to interest-based advertising.

By using our website, you consent to the use of these cookies in accordance with our privacy policy.

Why We Use Your Information?

We collect and use Information to analyze the usage of our Site, diagnose technical issues, and improve our services.

Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics, a third-party service, to assist us in understanding how visitors use the Site. You can learn more about how Google uses your Personal Information here: Google Privacy & Terms. You can also opt-out of Google Analytics here: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Microsoft Clarity

We use Microsoft Clarity to better understand how you use and interact with our website. This helps us improve our products and services, and lets us provide you with a better experience. We do this by using things like behavioral metrics, heatmaps, and session replay. To collect this data, we use both first-party and third-party cookies, along with other tracking technologies. This helps us see which of our products and services are popular, and it gives us insights into how people are engaging with our site. Plus, we use this information to make our website work even better and to keep it secure from fraud and other security issues. If you'd like to know more about how Microsoft handles your data, you can check out the Google Privacy & Terms. You can also opt-out of Google Analytics here: Microsoft Privacy Statement.

No Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property, or safety.

Your Rights

If you are a European resident, you have the right to access personal information we hold about you and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us through the contact information provided on the Site.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

Contact Us
