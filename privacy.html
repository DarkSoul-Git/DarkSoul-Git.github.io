Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit the website https://darksoul-git.github.io/.

Last updated: September 30, 2023.

Personal Information We Collect

When you visit the Site, we automatically collect certain information about your device. We collect this data using cookies, log files, and other tracking technologies. This information may include your IP address, browser type, internet service provider, referring/exit pages, your operating system, date/time stamps, and related data. We refer to this automatically collected information as "Device Information."

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies on our website. Google Analytics cookies help us understand how you interact with our site. Google AdSense employs DoubleClick cookies to display relevant ads and limit ad frequency. Additionally, affiliate tracking cookies identify referrals from partner sites, ensuring proper crediting and bonuses. By using our website, you consent to the use of these cookies in accordance with our privacy policy.

How Do We Use Your Personal Information?

We collect Device Information to analyze the usage of our Site, diagnose technical issues, and improve our services. We use Google Analytics, a third-party service, to assist us in understanding how visitors use the Site. You can learn more about how Google uses your Personal Information here: Google Privacy & Terms. You can also opt-out of Google Analytics here: Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Sharing Your Personal Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties. This does not include trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or servicing you, as long as those parties agree to keep this information confidential. We may also release your information when we believe release is appropriate to comply with the law, enforce our site policies, or protect ours or others' rights, property, or safety.

Your Rights

If you are a European resident, you have the right to access personal information we hold about you and to ask that your personal information be corrected, updated, or deleted. If you would like to exercise this right, please contact us through the contact information provided on the Site.

Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

Contact Us

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by e-mail at padhupadhu10a@gmail.com or by visiting the Contact Us page in our website.
